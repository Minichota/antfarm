AntSim
-------
A 2d hands-off ant colony simulator
----------
Ants:
	id
	hunger
	health
	sex 
	color


Ant types:
Queen
Worker  (female)
Soldier (male)

Environment:
	hard dirt (no gravity)
	soft dirt (moveable and affected by gravity)
	grass
		grows and is eaten by other bugs (eventually maybe)

Marker/Pheremone
	detected by ants, signal "food" or "danger"

Bugs
	Caterpillar
		lots of hp
		lots of food
		slow
	Aphid
		fast
		not much hp
		not much food
